Site Purpose

The Global Trip Planner website is designed to simplify travel planning for users. It offers services such as personalized itineraries, real-time flight and hotel recommendations, and local event guides. The purpose is to provide an all-in-one platform where travelers can access essential information for organizing their trips, view destination reviews, and save itineraries for future reference.

Visitor Scenarios

  • What are the top-rated attractions in my destination?
  • What are the best budget-friendly flights and accommodations for my trip?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #0B1665 (Midnight Blue) - Used for navigation links, buttons, and accents.

Secondary Color: #432534 (Light Chocolate Cosmos) - Used for headings, highlights, and calls-to-action.

Background Color: #F7F8FA (Light Gray) - Used for the page background to ensure readability and contrast.

Typography

Primary Font: 'Poppins', sans-serif

  • Headings: 'Poppins', 600 weight (bold)
  • Body Text: 'Poppins', 400 weight (regular)

Wireframe

A wireframe is a blueprint of the layout and structure of the website. It outlines where elements like navigation, images, and content will be placed. The mobile view wireframe will prioritize vertical stacking of content for ease of navigation on smaller screens.
